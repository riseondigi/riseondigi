It should follow a sequence.

What you have seen is mostly this—a copy that has program details, why the audience should join, about the person, and FAQs.

Now imagine a sequence where you add this:

Challenge Their Beliefs → Leave Them Hungry For More → Inspire Action

The first one might get readers to visit your page, but it’s the second one that will get them to convert!

Keep the audience’s needs, desires, and
remarkable solutions in mind.

If you’re telling your audience what they already know, or what they can get to know from elsewhere—why would they be tempted to buy
