Why do businesses fail to achieve “Digital Dominance”?

Strategy & End Goal do not align

Dominance over a space requires strategy more than execution. The right strategy will take you places that your action won't!

ZERO algorithm knowledge

Every platform has its own algorithm, even Google and Facebook. To be able to dominate those, one needs the CORRECT strategy of overpowering those algorithms.

Sell problems, people will come looking for solutions

People sell their product and talk about themselves more than talking to the customer about their problems. People want to know you less and want more of what you have to offer.

Less attention given to organic ways

Working on paid ads gets results, but that will stop once you stop putting in money. You develop when your brand develops, not when your ads run.

Not tracking the data

The BIGGEST problem why people don't achieve dominance is they don't track how they perform. It is HIGHLY important to customize your ways of work based on your performances.
